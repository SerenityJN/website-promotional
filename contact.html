Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements include a completed application form, Form 138 (Report Card), two 2x2 ID photos, and a certificate of good moral character. Please visit our Admissions page for complete details.

What academic tracks do you offer?

We offer four academic tracks: Academic Track (ABM, HUMSS, STEM), Technical-Vocational-Livelihood Track, Sports Track, and Arts and Design Track.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer various scholarship programs for academically excellent students, athletes, and those with financial needs. Contact our admissions office for eligibility requirements.
