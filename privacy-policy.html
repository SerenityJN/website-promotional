Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

Southville 8B Senior High School ("we," "our," or "the School") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of the personal information of our students, parents, and visitors. This policy explains how we collect and use your data in compliance with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173).

1. Information We Collect

When you use our website or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name and contact details (email address, phone number).
  • Academic information for admission inquiries.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process enrollment and admission applications.
  • Respond to your inquiries sent through the contact form.
  • Send important school announcements and updates.

3. Data Protection

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Access to personal data is restricted to authorized school personnel only.

4. Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data held by the school. For any privacy concerns, please email us at sv8bshs@gmail.com.
